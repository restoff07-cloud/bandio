How do I pay? ▼
Click "Get Premium" or "Get Intensive". You will see our Uzcard number to transfer to. After paying, send a screenshot of the transfer to our Telegram @abduganiev9009 along with the email you used to register. Your account will be upgraded within 24 hours.
How accurate is the AI band score? ▼
Our AI is trained on thousands of IELTS essays and calibrated against the official IELTS marking criteria. Scores are typically within 0.5 bands of a human examiner. It is the best preparation tool available, though the official exam is always marked by a certified examiner.
What is included in the AI writing feedback? ▼
You receive a band score for each of the 4 official IELTS criteria: Task Achievement, Coherence & Cohesion, Lexical Resource, and Grammatical Range & Accuracy. Each criterion includes specific, actionable feedback and suggestions to improve your score.
Is there a free trial for Premium? ▼
The Free plan gives you access to all practice materials without a time limit. Premium features like AI feedback are available immediately when you upgrade. Contact us on Telegram if you have any questions.
What is the difference between Premium and Intensive? ▼
Both plans include AI writing and speaking feedback. Intensive gives you more than double the monthly usage (65 writing feedbacks vs 30, and 350 speaking minutes vs 150), plus priority Telegram support from the teacher and exam strategy sessions — ideal if your exam is coming up soon.
