The story behind Bandio
Asilbek Abdug'aniyev
Founder of Bandio · English Teacher

When I was preparing for my own IELTS exam, I faced a problem I couldn't solve: there was no platform that let me practice in a real exam environment. Every mock test I found was either a PDF with no interactivity, a low-quality website with a confusing interface, or simply didn't exist in Uzbekistan at all.

I couldn't practice my writing and get real feedback. I couldn't do a full listening test with proper audio. I couldn't build my vocabulary systematically. I was preparing blind — and I knew thousands of students in Uzbekistan were facing the same problem.

So I decided to build the platform I wished had existed when I needed it. Bandio is the result — a complete IELTS learning environment where students can practice all four skills, take full mock tests with the exact same interface as the real exam, get AI-powered writing feedback, and build vocabulary through games.

My goal is simple: to give every student in Uzbekistan the tools to reach their target band score, regardless of where they live or how much they can spend.

"I built Bandio for the student I once was — determined, but without the right tools. You deserve better than a PDF mock test."
